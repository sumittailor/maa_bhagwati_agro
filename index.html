About Us

Our Story

Maa Bhagwati Agro was established with a vision to provide high-quality organic produce while promoting sustainable farming practices. Located in the fertile lands of Diwal Pandhana, Khandwa, we take pride in our commitment to quality and environmental responsibility.

Our journey began with a small farm and has grown into a trusted name in organic farming and export. We believe in the power of nature and work in harmony with it to deliver the best products to our customers.
