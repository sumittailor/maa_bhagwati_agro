Our Story

About Maa Bhagwati Agro

Our journey of passion, dedication, and growth

Our Humble Beginnings

Maa Bhagwati Agro was founded in 2010 by brothers Rajendra Kathore and Mahesh Kathore, sons of Nemi Chand Kathore. What started as a small family business in Diwal Pandhana, Khandwa, has grown into a trusted name in organic farming and export.

Inspired by their father's dedication to farming and with the blessings of Maa Bhagwati, the brothers began with a vision to provide chemical-free, high-quality agricultural products. Their commitment to organic practices and sustainable farming has been the cornerstone of their success.

From the initial days of working on a small piece of land to now managing extensive farms, Maa Bhagwati Agro has always prioritized quality over quantity. The brothers' hands-on approach and dedication to their values have earned them the trust of clients both locally and internationally.

2010

The Beginning

Rajendra and Mahesh Kathore started with a small plot of land, focusing on organic wheat and soybean cultivation. They faced numerous challenges but remained committed to their vision of chemical-free farming.

2012

First Expansion

After two years of hard work, the brothers expanded their operations to include onion cultivation and chili seed production. Their reputation for quality began to grow in the local market.

2015

Diversification

Maa Bhagwati Agro ventured into grape cultivation with a focus on export-quality produce. They also began offering agro consultancy services to help other local farmers adopt organic practices.

2018

Recognition

The business received certification for organic farming practices and began exporting grapes to international markets. This was a significant milestone for the company.

2023

Present Day

Today, Maa Bhagwati Agro is a recognized name in organic farming, exporting products to several countries while maintaining their commitment to quality and sustainability.
